There are 7 primary musical notes: C4, D4, E4, F4, G4, A4, B4
These 7 are called the major scale
General info: http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Note
all Hz frequencies: http://www.phy.mtu.edu/~suits/notefreqs.html

Can use 5 primary notes C4, D4, E4, G4, A4
these 5 are the http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Pentatonic_scale

LilyPond is a text-based music engraver; it is more similar to a programming language than a graphical score editing program. http://www.lilypond.org/website/windows.html
in LilyPod the pentatonic major scale is
<score vorbis="1">\relative c' { \time 5/4 c d e g a }</score>
